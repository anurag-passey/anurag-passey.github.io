Introduction

My name is Anurag Passey. I am a highly motivated professional seeking to enhance my knowledge and competence in the field of Data Science. In the intricate and interconnected world of international businesses, education, politics or any other field for that matter, the science of Data inundates our lives on day to day basis. This inspires me to earn aptitude and prowess in the field. Staggering volume of existing data, the ever-accelerating rate of the current & future data genesis and the potential for automation captivates me immensely to use my analytical and technical skills for advancement of information intensive industries. Right from the initial years of my middle school I was curious about the workings of a computer and always used to dig deeper into any relevant knowledge source to understand the technicalities of the computer system. Being a meritorious student at school and foreseeing promising prospects in the field, I was encouraged to pursue an undergraduate degree in Computer Science from the renowned college of my home country. Following my bachelors, I got a promising opportunity to work for Accenture which is which is a leading consulting corporation. To take my education and learnings to the next level I decided to join Conestoga college and I am confident in my preparations and determined to achieve my career goal. I have never allowed myself to be consumed completely by books. Apart from having good scholastics record, I have also amassed a rich experience in numerous extracurricular events ranging from participating to organizing multiple cultural & sports events and technical workshops.

Why Hire Me

I am a hardworking professional possessing a versatile skill set honed by industrial experience, uncompromising work ethic and positive outlook and I believe all these abilities of mine indicate that I will prove out to be a critical resource for your company. I feel that this position of business/data analyst brings a perfect opportunity for me to apply myself and invoke these qualities. Since my college days I have been passionate about all my projects and with consistent effort I have always managed to perform tremendously well in all of them. Not only do I focus just on my individual growth I tend to contribute towards the overall development of the team. Being a sports enthusiast I have always appreciated the importance of team work and as I acknowledge that the person performing the roll of business analyst needs to co-ordinate with different teams so I believe I will be a perfect fit for this position.
Adding to this, since I have entered this field, I have a deep sense of respect and admiration for the work delivered by your company and I have been diligently following and researching your company website and publications. This habit has developed in me a sense of attachment towards this company. Also, I have a unique set of qualities that separate me from the other candidates. I always try to look at the big picture. Not only do I try to complete the assigned work effectively, but I also try to be alert towards any opportunities that can maximize my potential. This ability to always try and push the envelope is something that is rare, especially in the people at initial stages of their careers and I possess this opportunistic nature in plenty.
